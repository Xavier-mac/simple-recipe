Stir-Fried Noodles

This quick and flavorful Stir-Fried Noodles recipe is inspired by Asian street food and is perfect for a busy weeknight meal.

Ingredients
Ingredient Quantity Substitute
Noodles 200g (rice noodles or egg noodles) Spaghetti
Soy Sauce 2 tbsp Tamari (gluten-free)
Garlic 3 cloves, minced Garlic powder
Vegetable Oil 2 tbsp Sesame oil for extra flavor
Vegetables (optional) 1 cup (mixed bell peppers, carrots, and cabbage) Any stir-fry vegetables
Protein (optional) 200g (chicken, shrimp, or tofu) No substitute needed

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Add the protein (if using) and cook until browned. Ensure the meat is fully cooked before adding the vegetables!
  5. Add the vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until they are slightly tender but still crisp.
  6. Add the cooked noodles and soy sauce, tossing everything together to coat the noodles evenly.
  7. Stir-fry for an additional 2 minutes, then remove from heat and serve.

    Serving Tips

    Serve the noodles with a wedge of lime and sprinkle with chopped green onions and sesame seeds for added flavor. For a spicy kick, add some chili flakes!
